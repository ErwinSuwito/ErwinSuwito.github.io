Our Mission

Our mission is to promote student wellness. We accomplish our mission by providing fellow students with activities for the body and mind.

Our Vision

Our vision is to utilize the recreational activities space that the university has provided and design activities that promote student health and wellness.

Our Objectives

Our objectives are to promote awareness about the facilities that the university has provided and the need to live a healthy lifestyle.

Who We Are

We are a group of people with an interest in Futsal at Asia Pacific University. We regularly meetup to play and train futsal together. We also want to raise awareness about the importance of living an active and healthy lifestyle..

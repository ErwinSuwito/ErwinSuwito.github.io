A New Website for the Club


Dear fellow members and others,


Welcome to the new APU Futsal Club website! This website offers users a more user-friendly way of getting the latest updates and stories from the club. From this day, all events, photos and updates will be published on this website. The current group chat will only be used for discussion and announcing very important announcements that will be posted both in the website and the group chat. We are in the process of moving the pictures from the group chat to this website, and this may take a few months as we'll also write a write up for the pictures as well. This new website also serves as a way for non-member to apply to be a member of the club.



As always, we're happy to receive any feedback and enquiries. Please explore the website and drop feedback about the new website in the contact us page.


From the desk of Club President.

New Recreational Activities Center

The University is now building a new recreational activities center beside the university. Currently, there are no integrated recreational activities center in the campus, every court for different sports are spread around the university area. The new recreational activities center will provide in all-in-one activities center where different sports courts and swimming pool will be located in one big building near the campus. The building will have one outdoor swimming pool, one indoor swimming pool, an indoor gym, two football field, three futsal fields, one basketball court, three multifunction halls, locker and showering areas, and clinics.

Due to be completed in 2022, the University has published several concept photos. You can see the photos below.
